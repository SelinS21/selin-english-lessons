About SEL-ENG

Hello and welcome! I'm Selin, a CELTA-certified English teacher specialising in teaching English as a second language to children aged 6–12.

I have over seven years of teaching experience and believe children learn best when lessons are engaging, interactive and enjoyable.

My aim is to create a positive learning environment where students feel confident speaking English while developing grammar, pronunciation and communication skills.

Every lesson is personalised to the learner's needs, helping children make steady progress while enjoying the learning process.

Teaching approach

Interactive

Activities designed to keep children motivated.

Supportive

A friendly environment where learners feel comfortable.

Personalised

Lessons adapted to each child's ability and goals.

Policies

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Lessons cancelled at least 48 hours in advance may be rescheduled.

Cancellations made with less than 48 hours notice may not be refunded.

If a student does not attend a scheduled lesson, the lesson may be considered completed after 10 minutes.

If I need to cancel a lesson, it will be rearranged at a convenient time.

Privacy Policy

SEL-ENG English Lessons respects your privacy. Information collected may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Lesson information

Payments are processed securely through third-party payment providers. Payment details are not stored by SEL-ENG.

Terms of Service

By booking lessons, parents or guardians agree to the lesson schedule, payment terms and cancellation policy.

Lessons are educational services provided online by SEL-ENG English Lessons.
